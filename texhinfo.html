About Benue Tech Skills

Benue Tech Skills is a program organised by the Benue State Government, in conjunction with Troika Tex Digital Communications Ltd., BishBery Digital solutions Ltd., Google and Microsoft.
The program is aimed at providing Tech Skills/creating employment opportunities for youths, with international certification.
Courses for the training include; Digital Marketing, Front end and back end development, Advanced Data analytics, e.t.c
